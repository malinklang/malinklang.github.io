About

I'm a 28 year old UX student living with my husband and cat in Bunkeflostrand on the outskirts of Malmö. I'm currently studying at EC utbildning while working part time in retail, specifically at Dressman.

Journey to UX

For the last couple of years I have worked in every imaginable sector of customer service; everything ranging from restaurants to hotels, stores, cafes, as well as a stewardess for SAS. I'd like to think that I've met an immense amount of different people and helped them in their various situations through my different roles and companies. It has, without a doubt, increased my understanding in terms of providing the best service based on the individuals personality and personal needs. Whether people are on a plane on their way to their yearly vacation, in a clothing store shopping for their kids, or on a website struggling to make the perfect purchase, they all have the commonalities of wanting to be heard, respected and having their needs met effortlessly. This is the beauty of UX design, I get to do just that but this time behind the scenes where I have ability and passion to ease the lives of people without them even realizing it.
