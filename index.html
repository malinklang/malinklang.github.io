About

UX Design Student

I'm a 28 year old UX student living with my husband and cat in Bunkeflostrand outside of Malmö. I'm currently studying at EC utbildning while working parttime as a salesperson for Dressmann XL.

Up until now, I worked in every imaginable sector of customer service; everything from restaurants to hotels, stores, cafes, airports, as well as on airplanes. I like to think that I've met people in almost every aspect of their daily life, which in turn has given me great value in terms of understanding what people want. Although everyone is different, we all want the same things, wether we are at a restaurant with our family or at an airplane heading for a business meeting. We want things to go smooth, to be effortless, and if problems arise, we want someone to hear us out and give us a simple solution. For me, UX does the same thing. Things should work, be effortless, and if problems arise, they should be solved in the simplest manner.
